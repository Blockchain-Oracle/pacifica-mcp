# Your First Trade

This guide walks you through a complete trading workflow using Pacifica MCP in Claude.

## Prerequisites

- Pacifica MCP installed ([see Installation](/getting-started/installation))
- A Pacifica account with USDC deposited (testnet or mainnet)

If you're starting fresh, use [testnet](/guides/testnet) — it's free.

---

## Step 1: Check market prices

Start by looking at the market you want to trade.

**Prompt:**
> *"What's the current price and funding rate for BTC-PERP?"*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-prices` and returns the mark price, oracle price, funding rate, and next funding time.

**Example response:**
```
BTC-PERP:
  Mark price:     $67,420.50
  Oracle price:   $67,415.00
  Funding rate:   0.0023% (every 8h)
  Next funding:   in 3h 12m
  24h volume:     $142M
  Open interest:  $89M
```

---

## Step 2: Check the order book

Before placing a market order, check liquidity.

**Prompt:**
> *"Show me the BTC-PERP order book — top 5 levels."*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-orderbook` with `agg_level=100`.

---

## Step 3: Check your account

Make sure you have enough margin.

**Prompt:**
> *"What's my account balance and available margin?"*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-account` and returns your equity, balance, margin used, and available margin.

---

## Step 4: Place a market order

Now place a long position.

**Prompt:**
> *"Buy 0.01 BTC-PERP at market price."*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-market-order` with `side=bid`, `amount=0.01`, `symbol=BTC-PERP`.

The MCP server signs the request with your Ed25519 wallet and submits it to Pacifica.

**Example response:**
```json
{
  "order_id": "ord_abc123",
  "symbol": "BTC-PERP",
  "side": "bid",
  "amount": "0.01",
  "filled_price": "67421.00",
  "status": "filled"
}
```

---

## Step 5: Check your position

Confirm the position is open.

**Prompt:**
> *"Show me my open positions."*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-positions`.

**Example response:**
```
BTC-PERP LONG
  Amount:       0.01 BTC
  Entry price:  $67,421.00
  Mark price:   $67,450.00
  Unrealized PnL: +$0.29
  Margin:       $67.42
  Leverage:     10x
```

---

## Step 6: Set a stop-loss

Protect your position with a stop-loss.

**Prompt:**
> *"Set a stop-loss on my BTC-PERP long at $65,000."*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-set-tpsl` with `symbol=BTC-PERP`, `side=bid`, `stop_loss_price=65000`.

---

## Step 7: Close the position

When you're ready to exit:

**Prompt:**
> *"Close my BTC-PERP long position."*

**What happens:** Claude calls `pacifica-market-order` with `side=ask`, `reduce_only=true`, and the same amount as your position.

---

## Tips

- Use **testnet** to practice without risking real funds — [Testnet guide](/guides/testnet)
- All amounts are decimal strings (e.g., `"0.01"` not `0.01`)
- Claude will always ask for confirmation before placing or canceling orders
- Check `pacifica-markets` for the exact symbol format (e.g., `BTC-PERP`, `ETH-PERP`, `SOL-PERP`)
